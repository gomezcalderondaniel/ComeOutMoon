Come out,

Moon!

A Children's Poem

read book

Night, night, the mighty night is here, All the creatures of the swamp are near. - Mighty, mighty, the mighty moon, All its glory it will show soon.

read book

With a cheer and a song the heaven wakes, For the air moves to the sound the swamp makes. - Moon! moon! The moon is full! Behind a cloud its shining is dull.

read book

A frog starts the song. With a croak it sings along. - "Come out, Moon! Come out! Are you ready to play?" - The moon replies with joy and lure: "I am doing my chores, have you done yours?""

A cricket follows the song. With a chirp it sings along. - "Come out, Moon! Come out! Are you ready to play?" - The moon replies with joy and lure: "I am brushing my teeth, have you brushed yours?"

As the sweet night goes along, All creatures of the swamp join the song. - "Come out, moon! Come out! Are you ready to play? We have done our chores for the day!"
